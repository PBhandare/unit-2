Understanding Wisconsin Climate Normals

This interactive map visualizes 30-year climate normals across Wisconsin, summarizing average temperature, maximum and minimum temperature, precipitation, and snowfall for each month. The proportional symbols show the magnitude of each variable at individual ZIP codes, with color coding to distinguish precipitation (blue), snowfall (white), and temperature (orange). The data come from the Wisconsin State Climatology Office, which aggregates historical observations from the National Climatic Data Center, and ZTCA datasets. Use the month slider and variable selector to explore how these climate patterns vary across the state and throughout the year.
